Baked Chicken

Simple but unbeatable.

Baked Chicken makes an easy but delicous night

Ingredients:

  • Chicken Breast
  • Brown Sugar
  • Seasoning
  • Butter
  • Olive Oil

Steps:

  1. Place the chicken breasts between two pieces of plastic wrap. Using a rolling pin or the flat side of a mallet, gently pound them to even thickness, about 0.5 to 0.75 inches.
  2. Rub the chicken breasts all over with olive oil. Combine all of the seasoning and the brown sugar in a small bowl and sprinkle on both sides of the chicken.
  3. Place the chicken in a 9x13 caserole dish.
  4. Top each chicken breast with a dab of butter, then bake for 18-20 minutes, until the internal temperature reads 165Â°F. I like to turn on the broiler for the last 2-3 minutes to add some nice browning, just watch closely so it doesn't burn!
